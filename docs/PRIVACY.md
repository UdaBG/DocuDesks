# DocuDesk — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 8 July 2026**

DocuDesk (and DocuDesk Lite) is a free application for signing and editing PDF
documents, available for Windows and Android. This policy explains, in plain
language, exactly what the app does with your information.

## The short version

**DocuDesk does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything
happens on your device.**

- No account and no sign-in.
- No data ever leaves your device — the app makes no network connections to
  provide its features.
- No analytics, no tracking, no advertising, no third-party data SDKs.
- **Completely free**: no ads, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, no
  payments of any kind.
- Your documents, signatures, and settings stay on your device.

## What the app works with (all on your device)

- **PDF documents you open** — processed locally to preview, edit, and sign.
  They are never uploaded anywhere.
- **Signatures you create** — drawn, photographed, or typed. Stored in the
  app's private storage on your device so you can reuse them. Never
  transmitted.
- **App settings** — such as your chosen interface language. Stored locally.
- **Files you save** — signed or edited copies are written only to the
  location you choose (or the app's own storage on mobile). Your original
  files are never modified.

## What the app does NOT do

- Does not collect personal information of any kind.
- Does not send your files, signatures, or any usage data over the internet.
- Does not use analytics or crash-reporting services.
- Does not display advertisements.
- Does not contain in-app purchases and does not process payments.
- Does not use cookies or web tracking.

## On-device processing

Some features do work that other apps send to a server — DocuDesk does it
locally instead:

- **Text recognition (OCR)** for scanned documents runs entirely on your
  device using a bundled, offline engine.
- **Removing password protection** from a PDF (so you can edit or sign it)
  runs entirely on your device.

No document content is ever sent anywhere for these features.

## Permissions

- **Android** — the app requests no sensitive or personal-data permissions and
  has no internet access at all. It reads only the specific files you pick
  through Android's own document picker, and writes only where you choose to
  save.
- **Windows** — standard desktop file access, limited to the files and folders
  you open or save to.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children, and it collects no data from anyone —
children included.

## Third-party components

DocuDesk bundles open-source components (for example the PDF, OCR, and font
libraries listed in the app's in-app *Licenses / Attributions* screen). These
run entirely on your device and do not collect or transmit any data.
